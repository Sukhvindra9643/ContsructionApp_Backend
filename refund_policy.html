3. Assessment and Processing of Refunds:

Construction Materials: Once we receive your refund request, our team will assess the condition of the materials returned. 4. Non-Refundable Items or Services:

Custom Orders: Any construction materials or services that were custom-made or specifically ordered for your project may not be eligible for a refund unless they are found to be defective or not as described.

Perishable Items: Perishable construction materials, such as certain adhesives or coatings, may not be eligible for a refund due to their limited shelf life.

5. Cancellation Policy:

Construction Services: If you need to cancel a construction service, please refer to our terms of service or contract for information regarding cancellations and any associated fees.

6. Damaged or Defective Items:

If you receive construction materials that are damaged or defective, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will work with you to resolve the issue, which may include a refund or replacement of the damaged items.

We reserve the right to modify or update this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on our website.
